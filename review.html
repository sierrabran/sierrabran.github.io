Hosts Review

First Website Host

godaddy.com

  • Cost: $2.99 for first year and $18.99 after that. Two year purchase required.
  • Host Services:GoDaddy offers things other than domains, such as: marketing tools and even ways to invest.
  • Domain Services: This website will host .com, .co, .site, .net, .xyz, .me, .shop, and .pro
  • Look of website:This website has a clean and organized look that seems to target people who are serious and may already know what they're doing.

This site would be my first option because the website really appeals to me and it has affordable options.

Second Website Host

hostgator.com

  • Cost: This website offers three different plans ranging from $2.75/month to $5.25/month.
    1. Hatchling Plan:
      • $2.75 per month.
      • Single website
    2. Baby Plan:
      • $3.50 per month.
      • Unlimited websites
    3. Business Plan:
      • $5.25 per month.
      • Unlimited websites
  • Host Services:HostGator offers shared hosting, website builing, and WordPress hosting.
  • Domain Services:This website offers all kinds of domains to choose from and you can even transfer one you already have.
  • Look of website:Nice looking website with a cute mascot. Looks like the website targets beginners and pros.

This site would be my second option because the website looks less serious and there are cheaper options on GoDaddy.

Third Website Host

siteground.com

  • Cost:This website also offers three different plans. They range from $3.99/month to $10.69/month.
    1. StartUp:
      • $3.99 per month.
      • 10 GB web space.
      • About 10,000 visits monthly.
    2. GrowBig (Best Seller):
      • $6.69 per month.
      • 20 GB web space.
      • About 100,000 visits monthly.
    3. GoGeek:
      • $10.69 per month.
      • 40 GB web space.
      • About 400,000 visits monthly.
  • Host Services:SiteGround has many hosting options that include: web, wordpress, woocommerce, cloud, and reseller hosting.
  • Domain Services:This website only had a few options for domain names and they seemed much more expensive than the other two websites.
  • Look of website:I don't like the look of this website as much as the others. Seems like it is marketed more for beginners.

This site would be my third option because it is more expensive and the website does not appeal as much to me.
